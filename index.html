.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Caltech, where I was advised by Adam Wierman. I am currently a TRIPODS Postdoctoral Fellow at Cornell University. I received a double B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering and Math at the University of Washington, where I worked with Maryam Fazel. My research interests are convex optimization and distributed algorithms.
